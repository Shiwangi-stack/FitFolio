DESCRIPTION

Men’s Sage Green Solid Velvet Shirt

Step into luxury with our Sage Green Velvet Shirt. Velvet is a fabric defined by its "nap"- a dense, soft pile that catches and reflects light to create a multi-toned effect. In this muted Sage Green shade, the fabric offers a sophisticated alternative to traditional black or navy, providing a calm yet commanding presence at any high-profile social gathering.

Key Performance 

  • High-Density Velvet Pile: Engineered for a soft, plush hand-feel that provides subtle warmth and a premium visual luster.

  • Muted Sage Green Pigment: A contemporary, earthy tone that stays saturated and rich, resisting the "ashy" look common in lower-quality velvet.

  • Regular Fit Silhouette: Carefully cut to provide a comfortable, relaxed torso while maintaining sharp, structured lines across the shoulders and collar.

  • Durable Construction: Built with reinforced seams and high-quality buttons to ensure the delicate velvet surface is supported by a rugged frame.

Style Guide: 

  • The Formal After-Hours: Pair this sage green velvet piece with charcoal grey trousers and black patent leather shoes for a look that is both modern and regal.

  • Modern Contrast: Wear it with our Black Slim-Fit Denim and Chelsea boots. The matte finish of the denim allows the velvet’s sheen to become the visual focus.

  • Layered Luxury: Wear it open over a white ribbed tank top for a more relaxed, "celebrity-inspired" weekend look.
